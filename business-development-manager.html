BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT MANAGER

An exciting and integral opportunity has arisen for a motivated Business development manager to join our fast-paced, high-growth business at a time of huge investment and development.

Responsibilities:

Being the initial point of contact for customers with queries about our products and services, in addition to following up regarding payments and appointments.

Key Role:
  • Responding to phone, email, and live-chat inquiries
  • Initiating a proactive approach with potential customers
  • Collecting interior design diagrams/measurements from customers to prepare and send them estimates
  • Confirming stock with warehouses
  • Dealing with procurement from partnering companies to fulfill customer requirements
  • Preparing estimates and following up on invoices
  • Collecting payments from customers
  • Establishing positive customer relationships
  • Using various technologies like CRM, WhatsApp, and similar other technologies related to the interior design industry
Additionally:

Candidates should be well-focused, proactive, courteous, and professional, provide excellent customer service abilities, and accomplish targets.

Preferred:
  • Knowledge of the interior design industry
  • Equivalent to native English communication skills (will not be compromised)
  • Experience with the UK and European customers
  • Good at numerics and software (will not be compromised)
  • Charming personality
In Return:

  • Salary hike for best performance

  • Job security
