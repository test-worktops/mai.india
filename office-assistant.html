OFFICE ASSISTANT

We are Looking for an Office Assistant with 2 years of Experience. The ideal candidate will be a hard-working professional able to undertake a variety of office support tasks and work diligently under pressure.

RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Managing office supplies.
  • Helping manage the office correspondence.
  • Monitoring the use of equipment and supplies within the office.
  • Assisting in menial office tasks required by the office staff both within and outside the office.
  • Assisting with various errands, such as grocery shopping, picking up supplies, or delivering documents.
  • Running general office-related errands, including but not limited to, pickups, courier services, and others.
  • Upkeeping general office cleanliness.
PREFERRED:
  • Activeness
  • Attentiveness
  • Politeness
  • Responsible attitude
  • English Communication is a bonus
MANDATORY:
  • Driving/riding skills
  • Driving license holder
